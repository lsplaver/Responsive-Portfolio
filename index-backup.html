About Me

A little about me. I have been interested in computers - hardware and software - for years. From early on I can remember trying out my dad's copy of VB6 and PowerBuilder programming environments and languages. Not long after I got interested in the hardware side which at that timee was primarily focused on figuring them out and how they worked. In school my interest grew and I started taking both hardware maintenance and software development courses.

I got into web development later at first as just a couple of courses but then more recently decided to go more in-depth with the courses.
